FALL_DETECTION.EXE

> Hospital Fall Detection

Falls are one of the leading causes of injury in hospital settings. Manual monitoring of patients — especially at night or in low-staff situations — is impractical. This system uses computer vision to automatically detect fall events and alert staff in real time.

🔍 How It Works

  • Video Feed Ingestion — A pre-recorded demo video loops continuously to simulate a live hospital camera feed.
  • Pose Estimation — Each frame is processed by YOLOv8-Pose, which detects human body keypoints (shoulders, hips, ankles, etc.).
  • Fall Logic — The system analyzes keypoint positions and angles to determine if a person has fallen (Torso becomes horizontal, center of gravity drops sharply).
  • Real-Time Streaming — Detected frames and fall events are streamed to the frontend via WebSockets.
  • Alert & Logging — Fall events are logged to MongoDB Atlas with timestamps for audit and review.

LEXAI.EXE

> LexAI — Legal Contract Intelligence

LexAI is an AI-powered legal contract analysis platform designed to help users upload complex legal documents and ask questions in plain English, receiving instant answers with page and clause citations along with structured risk assessments.

🔍 How It Works

  • Document Processing — Users upload PDF or DOCX contracts, which are parsed and chunked dynamically on the FastAPI backend.
  • Hybrid Vector Retrieval — Text chunks are converted to vector embeddings and stored in Qdrant Cloud for context-aware similarity searches.
  • RAG Pipeline Orchestration — LangChain coordinates query analysis and passes relevant contract clauses to Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model.
  • Structured Risk Evaluation — A specialized agent workflow analyzes the clauses to identify high, medium, and low-risk contractual clauses.
  • SSE Chat Streaming — Answers are streamed back to a modern React & Vite frontend using Server-Sent Events (SSE) for responsive UX.
